My design philosophy: the images, the fonts, the colors should add texture and depth to the words. Some mystery. The thoughtful choices seek to compel a person to pick it up, to linger, to wonder, to read.

Working as the sole designer for a bi-monthly magazine for moms, I honed my ability to tell stories in unique ways: a flowchart detailing one mom's struggles and successes at eating healthy, or a photo illustration to show the explosive topic of money.

For the same client, I collaborated with a team of editors to reimagine the structure of the magazine, redefine the tone and style, and source photography from readers whenever possible.
